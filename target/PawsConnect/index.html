Simple Process

How it works

1

Create Account

Sign up free with your phone number and email. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

Verify Identity

Sellers upload Aadhar ID. Our team reviews and approves within 24 hours.

3

GPS Photo Proof

Take a live photo using our in-app camera. Location and timestamp are auto-attached.

4

Go Live!

After admin approval, your listing goes live. Buyers can contact you directly.

Our Trust Technology

AI fakes don't
stand a chance

Our GPS camera is built right into the website. Photos are taken live — no gallery uploads allowed for listings — making fake AI-generated animal photos technically impossible.

📍
Location-stamped at capture

GPS coordinates are burned in at the exact moment the photo is taken — not editable after.

⏱️
Real-time timestamp

Date and time attached automatically. Pre-made or old photos cannot pass verification.

🔒
Gallery upload disabled

You must capture fresh, live. No uploading from storage — only live camera captures allowed for listings.
